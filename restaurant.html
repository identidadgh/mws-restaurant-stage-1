Tips for writing a good review

Before visiting restaurants, reading reviews online helps others make informed decisions about the place and discover interesting tips.

Regardless of the experience restaurant visitors had, reviews share useful insights and information about places. Here are some tips to write a great review:

  • To write a good review, describe what you experience.
  • Start with your arrival and end with your departure.
  • What do you see, what do you hear, what do you smell.
  • Keep your review relevant to the restaurant.
  • Don't get too personal.
  • Share a balanced perspective, positive and negative.
  • Your review should be credible and coherent.
  • Show courtesy and be respectful.
